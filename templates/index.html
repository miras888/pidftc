Who We Are

The PID Team is a group of young engineers from Almaty, Kazakhstan. With 11 members on board, we've united our efforts to bring innovative projects to life. Last season, three of our high school seniors competed as part of the QStem team in the FTC category, while four younger students engaged in the FLL category. This year, we merged forces to establish the PID team, combining our expertise and passion for technical challenges.
